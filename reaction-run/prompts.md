# Reef reaction animation prompt set

Generated with the built-in image generation tool from four supplied character
references. Each reaction was requested as a square 2x2 sprite sheet with four
sequential frames.

## Shared character and layout specification

- Use case: illustration-story
- Asset type: 4-frame animation sprite sheet for a chat sticker
- Input images: all four supplied images are character and style references for
  the same shark captain.
- Subject: the same mature muscular anthropomorphic blue tiger shark captain,
  with navy stripes, pale blue muzzle and chest, white naval captain hat with
  anchor badge, dark visor, sharp triangular teeth, and red-blue swim trunks.
- Style: polished cute expressive anime sticker illustration matching the
  references, crisp dark outline, glossy cel shading.
- Layout: one square 2x2 grid of four equal sequential frames, read top-left,
  top-right, bottom-left, bottom-right. Exactly one full character per frame,
  centered at identical scale and camera, whole silhouette visible, generous
  padding, fixed plain white background, and no panel borders.
- Consistency: preserve the same design, face structure, body proportions, hat,
  colors, stripes, outfit, lighting, and fixed camera across all frames. Only
  the requested action progresses. Frame 4 connects smoothly back to frame 1.
- Avoid: captions, speech bubbles, letters, watermarks, and extra characters.

## Action prompts

1. **舔舔舔:** playful half-lidded licking loop; tongue emerges, curls across
   the left side, sweeps across the center, then curls right and retracts. Only
   tongue, jaw, eyelids, and a small head bob move. Cute and non-explicit.
2. **揮螢光棒:** two bright cyan glow sticks move from low/outward to alternating
   raised arms, then a wide overhead V, and back through the opposite diagonal.
   Include short cyan motion arcs and glow.
3. **驚嚇到上下震動:** rapid up/down startled vibration with wide eyes, tiny
   pupils, hovering hat, hunched shoulders, trembling arms, and surprise marks.
4. **臉紅遮臉 不行不行:** cheeks redden, hands rise, face is covered while
   peeking through fingers, forearms cross into an X, then the face is covered
   again. Express the refusal only through body language.
5. **吐一口菸:** comedic weary-captain exhale with no smoking device. Cheeks
   puff, a small pale-gray ring appears, one rounded cartoon cloud drifts and
   curls upward, then fades into soft wisps.
6. **吐彩虹:** whimsical non-gory gag. Cheeks bulge, a short rainbow ribbon
   appears, a bright thick sparkling rainbow arc extends, then retracts.
7. **開心的搖尾巴:** huge warm closed-eye smile while the tail moves left,
   through center, right, and through center again; torso stays nearly fixed.
8. **醜哭:** lower lip trembles, tears well up, face scrunches, an uneven wail
   opens, then thick cartoon tears and droplets spray as the shoulders shake.
9. **色色吐舌頭＋流口水:** humorous non-explicit flirty loop with half-lidded
   eyes, rosy cheeks, tongue sliding out, glossy drool beads and strings, then
   tongue and drool retracting toward the first pose.
10. **阿~~~~（驚恐叫）:** terror shown without text. Eyes widen, jaw drops,
    hands rise, a wide scream and cheek-clutching peak with radiating sound
    lines, then a trembling gasp as the hat settles.
